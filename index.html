About Us

We are soldiers who start up. As veterans ourselves, we are dedicated to supporting the military community in starting and growing their own businesses. We understand the unique challenged faced by those who have served our country and are now seeking to transition into entrepreneurship. At Startup Soldiers, we are committed to honoring the sacrifice and service of our military members by providing them with the tools and support they need to succeed as business owners. Let's work together to help bring your ideas to life!

Serving all who served

Our Services

  • Business Plans

    We help you develop comprehensive business plans to turn your ideas into a successful reality.

  • Transition Assistance

    We help identify your goals to better understand if starting a business is right for you.

  • Funding Sources

    We connect veterans and servicemembers with financing options, such as grants and loans, to start and grow their businesses.

  • Virtual Workshops

    Our virtual workshops provide flexible and convenient training and education to help you succeed in your business ventures.

  • Market Research

    Our market research service provides valuable insights into your target market to help you make informed decisions and increase your chances of success.

  • Financial Cash Flow Projections

    Our financial analysis service provides a comprehensive analysis of your business's financials to help you make strategic decisions and ensure long-term success.
